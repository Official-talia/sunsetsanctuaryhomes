Services we offer

We offer a variety of services as stated

Medical and personal Care Services

We have trained medical staff, including registered nurses and licensed practitioners, who provide round-the-clock care to the seniors. They administer medications, monitor vital signs, and coordinate with physicians to manage seniors healthcare needs. the medical staff also help residents with activities of daily living,
as bathing, dressing, grooming, and toileting. They also provide mobility assistance for those with limited physical abilities.

Healthy and Nutritious Meals.

We typically offer three nutritious meals a day, along with snacks. Special dietary requirements and preferences are accommodated, and staff ensure residents receive appropriate nutrition based on their individual needs.We achieve this through the help of trained dietians and doctors.

Hospice and Rehabilitation services

We may offer palliative or hospice care services for residents who require end-of-life support. This includes
pain management, emotional support, and coordination of care to ensure a comfortable and dignified transition.:Our nursing home have rehabilitation departments that offer physical, occupational, and speech therapy services. These therapies help residents recover from injuries, surgeries, or illnesses, and improve their functional abilities.

ABOUT US

The Sunset Sanctuary Homes is a long-term care facility, and a residential institution that provides specialized care for elderly individuals who are no longer able to live independently. These facilities offer a range of services to meet the physical, emotional, and social needs of their residents. We,offer round-the-clock medical help to old people who need ongoing medical treatment and support
and seniors who are unable to take care of themselves because of physical or mental impairments like dementia, chronic illnesses, or other health issues.
